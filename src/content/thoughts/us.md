_Nov 5, 2023_

With your hands,

I write

With your blood,

I live

With your breath,

I breathe

With your body,

I do.
<br>
<br>
<br>
With these hands,

I fight

With this blood,

I stand

With this breath,

I transcend,

With this body,

I do.
